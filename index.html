About Me

I'm a cybersecurity professional with a deep passion for blue team operations — the side of security that catches the bad guys, not just looks for them. I hold an MS in Cybersecurity from the University of Missouri–St. Louis (GPA 3.8) and over two years of hands-on experience as a Security Analyst and Graduate Researcher.

My background spans SIEM monitoring, incident response, threat detection engineering, and vulnerability management. I've triaged hundreds of real alerts at AtlasProds Technologies and built detection pipelines from scratch using Security Onion, ELK Stack, and Splunk.

Outside of work, I run a personal security lab — building EDR tools, analyzing phishing traffic, and experimenting with detection rules. I believe the best defenders are the ones who never stop tinkering.

I'm from Kerala, India, currently based in St. Louis, MO, and actively looking for blue team roles — SOC Analyst, DFIR, Detection Engineering, or GRC/Compliance.
